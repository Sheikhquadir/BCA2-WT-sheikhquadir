About Our Department

Department of Computer Applications

The BCA programme at Mandsaur University is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in computer science, programming, and modern web technologies. Our curriculum blends theoretical knowledge with hands-on practical training, preparing graduates for the ever-evolving IT industry.

Established in 2010, our department has grown into one of the most sought-after programmes in central India. With experienced faculty, well-equipped labs, and industry collaborations, we ensure every student is career-ready upon graduation.

  • NAAC Accredited University
  • 100+ Placed Students Every Year
  • Industry-Aligned Curriculum
  • Dedicated Placement Cell
Our Vision

To be a centre of excellence in computer education, producing innovative and ethically responsible IT professionals who contribute to national development.

Our Mission

To deliver quality education through innovative teaching, practical exposure, and continuous skill development aligned with industry requirements.

Our Values

Integrity, innovation, inclusivity, and a commitment to nurturing well-rounded individuals ready to face global challenges.

Frequently Asked Questions

The Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) programme at Mandsaur University is a 3-year undergraduate degree divided into 6 semesters. Each semester includes theory subjects, practical labs, and internal assessments.

Candidates must have passed 10+2 (or equivalent) from a recognised board with Mathematics or Computer Science as one of the subjects, with a minimum of 45% aggregate marks (40% for reserved categories).

Semester II covers: Data Structures, Web Technology, Database Management Systems (DBMS), Operating Systems, and Mathematics-II. Each subject has corresponding practical labs.

Yes! Mandsaur University has a dedicated Training & Placement Cell that organises campus drives, mock interviews, resume-building workshops, and industry visits. Top recruiters include TCS, Infosys, Wipro, and Tech Mahindra.

During the BCA programme, you will learn C, C++, Java, Python, HTML/CSS/JavaScript, SQL, and more. The curriculum is updated regularly to include trending technologies.

Yes, separate hostel facilities are available for boys and girls within the campus. Hostels are equipped with Wi-Fi, mess, laundry, and 24/7 security. The annual hostel fee is approximately ₹45,000 – ₹60,000.

Absolutely! BCA graduates are eligible to pursue MCA (Master of Computer Applications), MBA, or other postgraduate programmes. Many students also opt for specialised certifications in cloud computing, data science, or cybersecurity.

The annual tuition fee for BCA is approximately ₹35,000 – ₹50,000 depending on the category. Scholarships are available for meritorious students and those from economically weaker sections. Contact the admission office for details.

The university organises various events including hackathons, coding contests, tech fests, cultural programmes, and sports tournaments. There are also active clubs for coding, robotics, photography, and debating.
